# Logo Files Needed

Place the following logo image files in this directory:

## Main Logo
- **bridgeview-logo.png** - Your Bridgeview Harbour Marina logo (shown at the top of the page)

## Platform Logos
- **feedback-icon.png** - Icon for the feedback form (could be a form/clipboard icon or your marina logo)
- **google-logo.png** - Google logo (download from Google Brand Resources)
- **marinas-logo.png** - Marinas.com/Dockwa logo
- **waterway-logo.png** - Waterway Guide logo
- **yelp-logo.png** - Yelp logo (download from Yelp Brand Resources)

## Image Requirements
- Format: PNG with transparent background (recommended)
- Main logo: Around 150x150px or larger (will be scaled down)
- Platform icons: Around 24x24px minimum, but larger is better (will be scaled appropriately)

## Where to Get Logos
- **Your marina logo**: Use your existing brand assets
- **Google**: https://about.google/brand-resource-center/
- **Yelp**: https://www.yelp.com/brand
- **Marinas.com/Waterway Guide**: May need to extract from their websites or contact them for official logos
